Jack Fox

Jack Fox is a punter in the National Football League (NFL) who currently plays for the Detroit Lions. He was signed by the Lions in 2020 and quickly established himself as one of the top punters in the league. Overall, Fox is widely regarded as one of the top punters in the NFL and a valuable asset for the Lions' special teams unit. With his talent and work ethic, he has the potential to continue having a successful career in the league.

Michael Badgley

Michael Badgley is a placekicker in the National Football League (NFL) who currently plays for the Detroit Lions. He was signed by the Lions in 2022 after spending three seasons with the Indianapolis Colts and one season with the Los Angeles Chargers. Overall, Badgley is widely regarded as a solid kicker in the NFL and a valuable asset for the Lions. With his accuracy, leg strength, and experience, he is poised to continue having a successful career in the league.

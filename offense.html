To be successful on offense, a team typically needs a strong quarterback who can make accurate and timely passes, a skilled offensive line that can protect the quarterback and open up holes for the running game, and talented receivers who can catch the ball and gain yards after the catch. It remains to be seen how the Detroit Lions offense will perform in 2023, as a lot can change in the world of professional football and this will be the team to do it.

Jared (the GOAT) Goff

Jared Goff is a quarterback who played for the Los Angeles Rams before being traded to the Detroit Lions in 2021. Goff was selected by the Rams with the first overall pick in the 2016 NFL Draft and spent his first five seasons with the team. During his time with the Rams, Goff showed promise as a young quarterback, leading the team to a Super Bowl appearance in the 2018 season. He has also been selected to the Pro Bowl twice in his career.

DeAndre Swift

DeAndre Swift is a running back who was drafted by the Detroit Lions in the second round of the 2020 NFL Draft. He played college football at the University of Georgia and was considered one of the top running backs in his draft class. Swift's strengths as a running back include his elusiveness, agility, and versatility. He has the ability to make defenders miss with his quickness and agility, and he is also a capable receiver out of the backfield.

David Montgomery

Montgomery had a solid rookie season in 2019, rushing for 889 yards and six touchdowns. However, it was in the 2020 season where he really shone, becoming a true workhorse for the Bears' offense. Montgomery is known for his physical running style, and his ability to break tackles and gain yards after contact. With his strong performances in 2020, Montgomery has emerged as one of the top young running backs in the NFL.

Amon Ra St.Brown

St. Brown is a talented receiver with good hands, precise route running skills, and the ability to make plays after the catch. He is also a tough and physical receiver who is not afraid to go over the middle of the field to make tough catches. He was a highly productive receiver at USC, finishing his college career with 178 receptions, 2,270 receiving yards, and 16 receiving touchdowns. With his talent and work ethic, St. Brown has the potential to become one of the top young receivers in the NFL.

Jameson Williams

Williams is known for his elite speed, explosiveness, and ability to stretch defenses. He has the potential to be a game-changing receiver at the next level, and his ability to create big plays could make him a valuable asset for any NFL team.
